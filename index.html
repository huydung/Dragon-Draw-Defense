How to Play

  • ⚓ Enemy ships arrive in waves — each wave is faster and brings more ships.
  • 🏝 Don't let 3 ships reach your island or the defense falls.
  • 🐉 Draw the shape shown on a ship's flag to command a dragon to destroy it.
  • ✍ The order of your strokes matters — watch the animated dots on the flag to learn the correct drawing sequence.
